A comprehensive ERP & Mobile solution designed to optimize maintenance and inventory for the mining industry.

Project Overview

The Challenge: Industries face significant wear and tear issues, necessitating frequent replacement of machines and spare components. It is crucial to determine the exact cause of wear to prevent production delays.

Previously, top mining firms relied on unstructured Excel spreadsheets to manage inventory. This was time-consuming and lacked clarity. Qualy5 developed an ERP dashboard and mobile application that gives supervisors a 360-degree overview, provides timely alerts, and saves significant effort.

Key Features:

  • Mobile & Tablet compatible (Android/iOS)
  • Cloud-based Data Storage
  • Real-time Wear & Tear Analytics

Challenges Faced

1. Legacy Paper Forms

Paper forms are difficult to maintain, prone to loss, and require manual data conversion. This often leads to missing data elements crucial for analysis.

2. Decentralized Excel Data

Data collected via Excel is often not centralized, making entry difficult. Analyzing and interpreting isolated spreadsheets is inefficient.

3. Lack of Visual Insights

It is difficult to visualize and interpret specific screen data points effectively when they are buried in rows of Excel data.

The Solution & Results

  • 1
    Mobile Application
    An app for tablets and mobile phones that permits quick identification and reporting of panel damage. Data is instantly stored in the cloud.
  • 2
    Cloud Management
    A service that manages and stores information, providing valuable deep insights into operational efficiency.
  • 3
    Smart Alerts
    Automated notifications for low stock levels and prompts for regular replacements.
