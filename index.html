Panel Tracking System

A comprehensive ERP & Mobile solution designed to optimize maintenance and inventory for the mining industry.

Project Description

It is crucial to determine the cause of wear and tear in mining operations. To prevent production delays, maintaining an up-to-date stock of spare parts and raw materials is essential.

Previously, top mining firms relied on unstructured Excel spreadsheets to manage inventory and wear data. This was time-consuming and lacked clarity. Qualy5 developed a robust ERP dashboard and mobile application that provides supervisors with a 360-degree system overview, timely alerts, and significant time savings.

Background

Iron ore is processed through large mechanical screens that remove large particles of ore from the finer dust. These screens are outfitted with panels that endure mechanical stress, fatigue strains, and damage caused by shocks or welding.

The Critical Need: It is vital to determine the exact location and cause of panel damage. By collecting and analyzing this data, mining companies can save thousands of dollars per month by identifying the source of harm early and minimizing its extent.

Challenges Faced

Legacy Paper Forms

Paper forms are difficult to maintain, prone to loss, and require manual data conversion. This often leads to missing data elements crucial for analysis.

Decentralized Data

Data collected via Excel is often not centralized, making entry difficult. Analyzing and interpreting isolated spreadsheets is inefficient.

Lack of Visibility

It is difficult to visualize and interpret specific screen data points effectively when they are buried in rows of Excel data.

The Proposed Solution

  • Module 1: Mobile App
    An app for tablets and mobile phones that permits quick identification and reporting of panel damage. All data entered is instantly synced to the cloud.
  • Module 2: Cloud Analytics
    A cloud-based service that securely stores information and processes it to provide valuable operational insights.
  • Module 3: Smart Alerts
    Automated notifications for low stock levels and prompts for regular replacements to prevent downtime.

Results Delivered

✓ Intuitive and secure software for daily operations.
✓ Clean, informative dashboard for quick decision-making.
✓ Proactive alert system before inventory runs out.
✓ Heat maps highlighting areas needing immediate attention.

Our solution utilizes Odoo for backend management and custom mobile interfaces for field data collection.
